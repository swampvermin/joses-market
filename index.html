Our Story

We are a family-owned grocery store in Wimauma, FL. Since 1993, we’ve proudly served our local community with authentic Latin American goods, fresh local produce, and a deli offering traditional Mexican dishes and desserts.

Menu

We offer a variety of hot foods daily including rice, beans, tacos, tortas, and our popular chile poblanos. Our daily menu changes regularly, so please call us to place an order or inquire about today’s specials. We also provide catering services for birthdays, weddings, and any special event.
